Lean product development

The Lean Startup approach is based on the premise that every innovative project is a grand experiment that attempts to answer this question: "Should this product be built?" and "Can we build a sustainable business around this set of products and services?" This experiment is more than just theoretical inquiry; it is a minimum viable product (MVP). If it is successful, it allows a manager to get started with his or her campaign: enlisting early adopters, adding employees to each further experiment or iteration, and eventually starting to build a product and a new business around it.
We help you build the MVP.
